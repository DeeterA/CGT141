Our Unique History
The Alpha Zeta chapter of Tau Kappa Epsilon is the longest running chapter in TKE history. Our chapter was founded on December 20, 1928, and we have not shut down since. We are one of only two chapters that kept our house during WWII. The only other chapter to keep their house during this period was the Alpha Phi chapter at the University of Kansas.
Our Mission Statement
We, the Alpha-Zeta Chapter of Tau Kappa Epsilon, strive to be the Fraternity for Life, aiding young men with their mental, social, physical, and spritual growth with a brotherhood based upon the principles of Love, Charity, and Esteem. We aim for our brothers to be quality citizens of the community and society.
