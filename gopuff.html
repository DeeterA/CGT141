TKE Alpha Zeta Partners with GoPuff on Purdue's Campus!

At the start of the semester, the Alpha Zeta chapter of Tau Kappa Epsilon partnered with GoPuff on their tour of college campuses nationwide. GoPuff, a convenience store delivery service much like postmates or hungry boiler, worked with our chapter on securing booths outside of high traffic

areas on campus to help spread the word of their new service. In return for our help, GoPuff matched every download of their app with $1 towards St. Jude, TKE's nationwide philanthropy. Check back soon to see the total amount raised once we get updates from GoPuff!
