Winterization 2016

On Saturday, Nov. 5 our brothers took part in the annual Purdue tradition of Winterization. Winterization is a community service project in which students from Purdue University assist in preparing the yards and homes of the elderly and disabled in Tippecanoe County for winter. We contributed to helping over 350 homes with nearly 1400 volunteers and would like to extend our appreciation to the Wesley Foundation for organizing another great campus wide community service project. Contact info@purduewinterization.org for more information about Winterization, and our Philanthrapy Chair, Jack Neidnagel, at jniednag@purdue.edu for more information on our efforts of community service as a whole.
